Unity3D - Fishingnesia

ADTPDN

A simple 3D fishing simulation game, I've created for my university thesis using Unity 3D, the game aims for presenting endemic fish species in Indonesia, by fishing in this game, you can get to know the species of fish found in the waters around Indonesia.

The game itself currently available for windows, you can check on my itch.io page
